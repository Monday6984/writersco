Our story

We grew into something bigger.

"We market. You grow." Photo of Testimony coming here

Founded by

Testimony Olajire

WritersCo was founded in 2021 out of a real and growing need. Clients needed written content. Freelancers needed coordination. And there was a gap between what businesses needed to say and the people who could help them say it well.

So we filled it.

What started as a writing and content operation quickly grew as our clients grew. They needed more than articles. They needed strategy, brand voice, SEO, books, websites, training, and a partner who understood their business well enough to speak for it. We grew to meet every one of those needs.

Testimony is a medical doctor turned entrepreneur who understands both the science of problem-solving and the art of communication.

His background in medicine shaped how WritersCo works: precise, thorough, outcome-focused, and genuinely invested in the results you walk away with.
